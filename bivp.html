Pioneering BIPV in Nigeria

Building-Integrated Photovoltaic Engineering

Metikon Engineering is the local partner in Nigeria's first Building Integrated Photovoltaic (BIPV) project. Unlike conventional solar panels, BIPV systems integrate seamlessly into building structures—roofs, façades, carports, and canopies—serving dual functions as both architectural elements and power generators. Our flagship Wattpod infrastructure transforms parking structures, fuel stations, and corporate campuses into energy-generating assets, combining aesthetic appeal with sustainable power delivery.
