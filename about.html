Who We Are

Africa-Born Infrastructure Innovators

Metikon Engineering Limited is an Africa-born infrastructure innovator at the frontier of BIPV engineering, distributed utilities, energy intelligence, and grid modernization. Established in 2001 with RC No. 417281, we design, deploy, and maintain power solutions that enable Africa's urban, rural, and industrial transformation.

We specialize in providing sustainable solutions to address energy access problems in Nigeria by leveraging our unique technical capabilities and deep understanding of the domestic energy market. With almost a decade of energy management experience for telecommunication tower infrastructure and having transformed into an integrated energy company, Metikon delivers innovative and novel sustainable energy solutions across every segment of the power value chain.

Our Journey

The Metikon Story

Founded in 2001, Metikon Engineering Limited began with a clear mission: to deliver world-class energy and engineering solutions that power business operations and improve economic outcomes for our clients. What started as a focused energy services provider has evolved into one of Nigeria's leading infrastructure engineering firms.

Our journey has been marked by significant milestones. We managed over six hundred telecommunication tower sites at our peak, providing fuel supply, distribution, and grid infrastructure development. This experience gave us unparalleled insights into Nigeria's energy challenges and opportunities.

Today, Metikon stands as a recipient of the Rural Electrification Agency's off-grid Solar PV Grant and has been shortlisted by the World Bank-assisted Nigeria Electrification Project (NEP). We are the local partner in Nigeria's first Building Integrated Photovoltaic (BIPV) project, demonstrating our commitment to pioneering innovative energy solutions across the continent.

Through strategic partnerships with global technology leaders and our relentless focus on engineering excellence, we continue to transform Africa's energy landscape—one project, one community, one innovation at a time.

Our Vision

To attain a sustainable leadership position in the energy and power sector with indigenous capacity and to be among the global frontiers through service delivery and reliability. We shall continue to grow our capacity to take full delivery of the most stringent solutions to address the energy challenges in Africa, while creating a green future.

Our Mission

  1. Engineering Excellence: To design, construct, operate and maintain the most efficient and economic energy facilities by integrating Total Quality Management, Cost Effectiveness and timely delivery into our solutions and services.
  2. Green Future: We pursue a green future and eco-friendly environment for a peaceful habitat. Our main focus is on energy efficiency and environmental sustainability.
  3. Sustainable Impact: We deliver exceptional products and unrivaled service that together deliver premium value to our customers while addressing Africa's energy access challenges.
