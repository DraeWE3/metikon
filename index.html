Frequently Asked Questions

What is BIPV and how does it differ from traditional solar?
Building Integrated Photovoltaic (BIPV) systems integrate solar technology directly into building structures like roofs, facades, and carports. Unlike traditional panels, BIPV serves both as building material and power generator, offering aesthetic appeal and structural functionality.
Can Metikon design solutions for rural communities?
Absolutely. We specialize in mini-grid development and solar home systems specifically engineered for rural electrification. Our projects like the Nsofang 117 kWp Mini-Grid demonstrate our commitment to energy access and socio-economic inclusion across Nigeria.
What industries does Metikon serve?
We serve commercial institutions, telecommunications infrastructure, municipal projects, industrial facilities, and residential communities. Our portfolio includes C&I installations, telecom mast management, public street lighting, and grid modernization projects.
Does Metikon provide maintenance after installation?
Yes. We offer end-to-end services including design, deployment, operations, and maintenance. Our team manages the entire lifecycle of energy projects with robust health, safety, and environmental policies ensuring long-term system reliability.
What makes Metikon different from other energy companies?
We combine over 20 years of local market expertise with global technology partnerships. As an engineering-first company, we deliver world-class solutions backed by OEM leaders like Solar Innova, Alpha ESS, and advanced monitoring systems—ensuring both technical excellence and financial sustainability.
